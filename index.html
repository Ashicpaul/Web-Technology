Animal Type

Examples: Lions, tigers, elephants, giraffes, etc.

Examples: Eagles, parrots, peacocks, penguins, etc.
Reptiles:
Examples: Snakes, lizards, crocodiles, etc.
Amphibians:
Examples: Frogs, salamanders
Fish:
Examples: Sharks, stingrays, angelfish, etc.

Habitats

  1. Forests:
    • Tropical Rainforests: Tigers, orangutans, monkeys
    • Temperate Forests: Deer, bears, wolves
    • Boreal Forests (Taiga): Elk, wolves, grizzly bears
  2. Grasslands:
    • Elephants, Bison, Antelope
  3. Oceans:
    • Dolphins, Sharks, Whales

Animal Diets

  1. Mammals

    Herbivores: Elephants, Deer
    Carnivores: Lions, Tigers
    Omnivores: Bears, Monkeys

  2. Birds

    Herbivores: Parrots
    Carnivores: Eagles, Owls
    Omnivores: Crows, Ducks

  3. Reptiles

    Carnivores: Snakes, Crocodiles, Lizards

  4. Fish

    Carnivores: Sharks, Piranhas
    Herbivores: Parrotfish, Surgeonfish
    Omnivores: Goldfish, Guppies

  5. Amphibians

    Insectivores: Frogs, Salamanders
    Tadpoles: Herbivores - eat algae
